Tasty Gourmet Sandwiches and Salads to put Your Hunger to rest in Omaha, NE

In the mood for scrumptious, gourmet sandwiches or tasty, gourmet soups? If you live in Omaha, NE, then visit Pickleman’s Gourmet Café for the delectable foods you can’t live without. Whether you need catering services for a business luncheon or you want your food delivered to your home, we’ll provide you with food that can satisfy any craving.

Browse Through Our Menu

Want to eat something a little greener for lunch? Choose from our wide selection of gourmet salads. Our options include Avo-Cobb, Italian Chop, and Buffalo Chicken. For a heartier meal choice, try one of our toasted sandwiches, made with white or wheat artisan bread. With our Pick 2 Combo, you can choose any combination of a half sandwich, a half salad, and an eight-ounce soup.

FRESH INGREDIENTS FOR DELECTABLE SANDWICH DELIVERY

Pickleman’s Gourmet Café is committed to providing our customers with delicious sandwiches using the freshest ingredients available. Join us at one of our locations or start your Order Online for superior delivery option near you!
